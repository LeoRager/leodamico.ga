HOW TO INSTALL

Works on iOS only

STEP 1

Install Shortcuts

Install Shortcuts from the app store. Here's the link: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/shortcuts/id915249334?mt=8

STEP 2

Install the shortcut

Click on this link: https://www.icloud.com/shortcuts/5fd86aaf35304c93970c01d0a3e0a12f and press on "Get Shortcut"

STEP 3

Add Siri functionality

This will have added "Menu" to your library. Go to your library and press on the three dots on menu. Then press on the control center on the top right of the screen. Next, press "Siri Phrase". Record what you want to say to trigger the shortcut. Press done. Press Done.
